### Amazon Case Study: Revenue +20%, Profit -10%

## 1. Problem Statement

Amazon's **revenue increased by 20%**, but **profit decreased by 10%** compared to the previous month.

Since:

**Profit = Revenue − Cost**

This indicates that **costs have increased faster than revenue**. The objective is to identify **which costs increased, where the issue occurred, and why.**

---

## 2. Clarifying Questions

- When did the profit decline start?
- Is the issue across the entire business or only specific product categories?
- Which product categories are generating lower profit despite higher sales?
- Which cost component increased the most (COGS, discounts, shipping, returns, marketing)?
- Is the issue concentrated in specific regions, fulfillment centers, sellers, or customer segments?

---

## 3. North Star Metric

**Profit Margin**

Why?

Revenue is growing, but profitability is declining. Profit Margin helps determine whether revenue growth is translating into healthy business performance.

---

## 4. Supporting Metrics

Since

**Profit = Revenue − Cost**

I would analyze:

- Revenue
- Profit
- Profit Margin
- Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
- Discounts & Promotions
- Shipping Cost
- Return Rate
- Refund Cost
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

---

## 5. Segmentation

To identify where profitability declined, segment the analysis by:

- Product Category
- Discount Level
- Shipping Method
- Fulfillment Center
- Seller Type
- Region
- Customer Type (Prime vs Non-Prime, New vs Existing)

*(Notice these are dimensions most closely related to profit.)*

---

## 6. Funnel (If Applicable)

Since revenue increased, the purchase funnel is likely **not the primary issue**.

Instead, I would focus on the **post-purchase journey**:

```
Purchase
     ↓
Shipping
     ↓
Delivery
     ↓
Return Request
     ↓
Refund
```

This helps identify whether returns, refunds, or delivery-related costs are reducing profitability.

---

## 7. Hypotheses

**H1:** High discounts increased sales but reduced profit margins.

**H2:** Return and refund costs increased significantly.

**H3:** Shipping and fulfillment costs increased.

**H4:** Certain product categories have high revenue but low margins.

**H5:** Product quality issues led to more returns and refunds.

---

## 8. Data Needed

To validate these hypotheses, I would request:

- Revenue by product category
- Profit by product category
- Profit Margin by category
- COGS
- Discount %
- Shipping Cost
- Return Rate
- Refund Cost
- Region-wise performance
- Fulfillment Center performance
- Seller Type performance

---

## 9. Analysis Plan

1. Validate the reported decline in profit.
2. Compare this month's metrics with the previous month.
3. Identify which cost components increased.
4. Segment the data to locate where the issue exists.
5. Test each hypothesis using relevant metrics.
6. Identify the root cause.
7. Recommend actions based on evidence.

---

## 10. Recommendations

Recommendations would depend on the identified root cause. Examples include:

- Review discount strategy for low-margin products.
- Improve product quality to reduce returns.
- Optimize shipping and fulfillment costs.
- Focus on high-margin product categories.
- Review supplier pricing if COGS has increased.
